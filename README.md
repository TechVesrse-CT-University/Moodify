Moodify uses your webcam to detect emotions and gestures live in the browser. When you smile, give a thumbs up, or look surprised, you earn "MoodPoints" and unlock rewards, badges, and streaks. Use cases: classrooms for engagement tracking, workplace wellness breaks, livestream audience interaction, or just a fun personal mood journal.
